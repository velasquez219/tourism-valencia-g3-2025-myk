Welcome to Valencia

Valencia is a refreshing mountain town just minutes from Dumaguete City. Known for its cool climate, lush greenery, and breathtaking natural wonders, it’s the perfect getaway for travelers seeking peace, adventure, and unforgettable scenery.

Cool Mountain Climate

Enjoy fresh air and relaxing weather, perfect for outdoor adventures or peaceful retreats.

Stunning Nature Spots

Home to Casaroro Falls, Pulangbato Falls, Forest Camp, natural springs, and more.

Eco-Tourism Haven

Valencia promotes green tourism, conservation, local culture, and sustainable travel.
